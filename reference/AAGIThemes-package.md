# AAGIThemes: AAGI Branding for Graphical and Tabular Outputs

Applies Analytics for the Australian Grains Industry ('AAGI') external
brand guidelines to graphics. 'AAGI' colours and font guidelines are
applied as useful and reasonable to base graphics, 'ggplot2' figures,
'flextable' and 'gt' objects.
